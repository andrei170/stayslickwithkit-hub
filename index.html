01

No VSL On The $4K Offer - Applicants Decide On Vibes Instead Of Story

Stay Slick With Kit is a $4K, 12-week 1:1 coaching offer. The page that sells it has reels, a pillar grid, and an application form - but no long-form video pre-framing the call. Applicants land cold, fill in 4 fields, and either show or don't. There's no philosophy walkthrough, no client-archetype calibration, no chance for Kit to filter belief before her calendar burns a slot. Close rates on cold-application calls hover at 25-30% when a 4-min "A Word From Kit" pre-frame would push them to 40-50%.

Cost of Waiting

~$6-12K/mo in coaching spots not closed because applicants arrived cold instead of pre-framed - ~$72-144K/yr compounding while applicants who should buy walk because the story didn't land in 4 minutes. Every cycle, the highest-LTV offer in the stack converts below its ceiling.

6 months from now ·~$36-72K never collected. Plus 6 more cohorts of applicants who decided "maybe later" because they never saw the version of you that closes in front of camera.

Fix · 4-min "A Word From Kit" VSL - cold open with the philosophy, the right archetype, and the screen mechanic. 1.5-2x close rate uplift on existing applications. $72-144K/yr Year-1 floor.
Fast Install
02

Form-To-Nothing On The Highest-Value Step - 44% Of Calls Cancelled Last Month

The application form on the live page is the highest-value moment in the funnel. Right now it's a Typeform with no confirmation page, no auto-email, no calendar handoff, no pre-call asset. 4 of 9 calls cancelled last month - a 44% drop. Applicants finish the form, get nothing back, doubt the moment, and ghost. The fix is plumbing - confirmation page + immediate calendar booking + pre-call email with one specific prep question.

Cost of Waiting

~$4-8K/mo in calls that cancel before they happen - ~$50-100K/yr in coaching spots burned because the post-form moment goes silent. The 44% no-show rate compounds: every cancel is a slot Kit can't refill same-week, calendar throughput keeps decaying.

6 months from now ·12-24 more calls cancelled at the same 44% rate, ~$24-48K never recovered. Calendar throughput will be 30-40% below where it should be on the same application volume.

Fix · Replace Typeform-to-nothing with Typeform → confirmation page → auto-Calendly handoff → pre-call email. Show-rate jumps from 56% to 80%+. ~$50-100K/yr recovered.
Compound Pickup
03

Hook-To-Offer Mismatch - "Why He Ghosted Me" Reels, $4K Coaching Offer

The top-performing reels are "why he ghosted me / how to read his texts" content. The 27K @stayslickwithkit audience that grew off those reels arrives expecting a free-content hot-take channel, not a $4K private coaching engagement for career-built women. The hook calibrates the wrong audience. Qualified Founder-tier applicants self-disqualify because the surface looks like commodity dating content. Kit has the credentials and the screen mechanic - the demo just doesn't match.

Cost of Waiting

~$5-10K/mo in qualified applicants quietly walking - ~$60-120K/yr in coaching pipeline never materialising because the hook attracts ghosting-content viewers, not the actual buyer. The right Founders are watching coaches whose demo matches their archetype.

6 months from now ·Another quarter of mismatched reels feeding the wrong audience, ~$30-60K never collected, and the qualified Founder-tier viewers who would have applied keep migrating to coaches whose hooks match theirs.

Fix · 60-hook bank pivoting toward Founder / Senior Operator / Reset archetypes. Existing reels stay; new reels pull qualified applicants. Year-1 floor $60-120K incremental.
