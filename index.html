The Three Bottlenecks

What's leaking, ranked by revenue impact.

Each one is observable, each one is fixable. The order matters: number one is the fastest revenue lever, number three is the slow compound that keeps the funnel filled with the right kind of buyer.

01

No VSL on the $4K offer

Your $500 course has a VSL. Your $4,000 1:1 doesn't. The funnel mechanics are inversely correlated with offer price. High-ticket buyers need certainty before they fill out a form, and a VSL is the cheapest way to deliver that at scale.

Highest Revenue Lever
02

Form-to-nothing on the highest-value step

4 of 9 calls cancelled last month. A 44% drop. Not a setter problem. A calendar problem. The form submits and dies. By the time anyone reaches out, the warmth has gone. Form auto-redirect to Calendly, plus a three-touch pre-call sequence, fixes this in two weeks.

Fast Two-Week Install
03

Demo mismatch between hook and offer

Your top reels are "why he ghosted me" content. Your offer is "find a high-quality man." Different buyers. The wealthy 30-45 demo doesn't engage with hooks aimed at 24-year-olds. A second content track plus a podcast guest pivot fixes it over 90 days.
