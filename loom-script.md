# Loom Script - Kit Esuruoso (Warm Intro Version)

> 3-4 min cinematic loom. NOT a cold-outreach loom. This is the leave-behind that goes with the hub URL after the 3-way call. Refers to specific things you discussed live so it lands like a follow-through, not a pitch.

---

## Tone

Direct, calm, professional. Andrei's voice. UK/Belfast inflection. No marketing energy. This is "I said I'd send the breakdown, here it is."

---

## Structure

### 0:00 - 0:15 - Open

**Bespoke. Re-record after the call.**

> "Kit, Andrei here. Just wrapped up our call. I've put together everything we talked about into a single hub - link's below the video. Wanted to walk you through it quickly so you can come back to it later if you need to."

If the 3-way call hasn't happened yet (sending this BEFORE the call as the intro), use:

> "Kit, Andrei here. [Mutual contact] connected us - looking forward to our chat. I put together a full breakdown of what I've found inside your funnel before we talk, so we're not starting from zero on the call. Walking you through the highlights now. Link to the full hub is below the video."

### 0:15 - 0:45 - Why I built this

> "Spent a few hours pulling apart your funnel front to back. Watched your VSL on `/cym-vsl`, walked through the application page, looked at the IG and the podcast, ran the math.
>
> Three things stood out. None of them are a question of effort - your front end is strong. They're all about plumbing on the back end. Specifically, the back end of your $4K offer."

### 0:45 - 1:30 - Bottleneck #1 (VSL gap)

> "First thing. You've got a VSL on the $500 course. You don't have one on the $4,000 1:1.
>
> That's backwards. The $4K is where a VSL would compound the most. High-ticket buyers need to feel certainty before they fill out a form. Right now your $4K page is asking them to commit cold.
>
> Inside the hub, there's a 4-minute VSL preview I drafted in your voice - direct, no fluff, hits the wealthy demo angle. It'd live on a `/1-1-vsl` page above the application form."

### 1:30 - 2:15 - Bottleneck #2 (sales process)

> "Second. The 4-of-9 cancellation issue.
>
> That's not a setter problem. It's a calendar problem. The form submits and dies - that's where the warm money is leaving. By the time anyone reaches out manually, half the people have cooled off.
>
> The fix is three layers - form auto-redirects to Calendly, three-touch pre-call sequence, and a setter playbook that stops them pitching and just protects the calendar. Full breakdown's in the hub. Show rate goes from 56% to 80%+ realistically."

### 2:15 - 3:00 - Bottleneck #3 (content / demo)

> "Third one's slower. Your top reels are 'why he ghosted me' content. Your offer is 'find a high-quality man.' That's pulling the wrong demo into the funnel.
>
> Hub's got a 90-day plan to add a second content track aimed at 30-45 professional women, plus a podcast guest pivot, plus a photoshoot brief. Doesn't replace what you're doing - adds to it.
>
> This one compounds slowly, but it's what keeps the $4K funnel filled with the right kind of buyer once we install the rest."

### 3:00 - 3:30 - What's in the hub

> "In the hub you'll find the full bottleneck analysis, a funnel blueprint with every phase mapped out, the VSL preview, the DM-setting playbook, the content strategy with hook bank, an email pre-call sequence, and a 90-day install plan.
>
> All of it's already done. You can pull pieces of it out independently if you want to start small."

### 3:30 - 3:45 - CTA

> "If you want to talk through which pieces to install first, calendar's in the hub. We can scope an install for you in 90 days - you keep filming, we plumb the back end.
>
> Either way, the work is yours. Use what's useful, ignore the rest. Speak soon."

### Visual recommendation

- Andrei centred, framed mid-chest up
- Clean background (bookshelf, minimal desk, or outdoor cinematic)
- Natural light, warm
- 4K, 16:9
- Single take preferred - feels less produced
- B-roll cuts when referring to specific URLs / sections of the hub (screen recording walking through them)

### Hosting

- Loom or Vimeo
- Embed at top of hub page
- Custom thumbnail with Kit's name on it ("For Kit - Andrei")
- Length cap: 4:00 max. Don't overrun.

| CTA (pre-recorded) | 0:15 | NO - reusable |

For Kit specifically: build as a Super Loom (fully bespoke) since this is a warm-intro 3-way call situation, not cold outreach. He's a top-20 prospect in the warm pipeline. Worth the extra 30 minutes.
